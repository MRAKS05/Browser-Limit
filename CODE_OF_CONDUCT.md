# Code of Conduct

## Our Purpose

Browser Limit exists to help individuals and families regain control over digital habits, overcome harmful browsing behaviors, and protect vulnerable users from inappropriate content. This project serves a serious purpose — addiction recovery, parental responsibility, and digital discipline.

All contributors and participants are expected to uphold the values that make this community effective.

## Expected Behavior

- Respect the project's mission of digital wellbeing, self-discipline, and parental control
- Communicate with maturity, civility, and constructive intent
- Respect others' privacy, especially regarding sensitive topics like addiction recovery
- Keep discussions focused on the technical and practical aspects of the project
- Acknowledge that different cultures, faiths, and personal values exist — respect them

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Sexualized content, language, or imagery of any kind
- Trolling, harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Mocking or belittling the project's purpose (addiction recovery, parental controls, digital discipline)
- Promoting or encouraging harmful browsing habits or bypassing parental controls
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Disruptive behavior that derails productive discussion

## Addiction Recovery Safe Space

This project supports users in recovery from compulsive internet use, including pornography addiction. Be mindful that:

- Discussions may involve sensitive personal experiences
- Judgment or ridicule of anyone's recovery journey will not be tolerated
- Sharing resources that undermine recovery efforts is prohibited

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will review reported violations and take appropriate action, which may include warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the community.

## Reporting

To report a violation, open a private issue or contact the repository maintainer directly through GitHub.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is written for the Browser Limit community and its mission.
